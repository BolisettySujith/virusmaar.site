About Our Game

In the stages of any kind of virus spread, doctors need to make a vaccine, so doctors need to test with many vaccines for discovering a cure for a virus, at that stage it becomes difficult for the doctors to give a proper vaccine to their patients though if the vaccine is discovered in some cases there will be problems asirining to provide the vaccine to the patients. So taking this part as an inspiration to provide vaccines to the virus infected patients in difficult situations. Virus Maar is an logical and strategic interactive puzzle game.

In this game, doctor avatar need to play to complete 2 seasons each of 5 levels to complete the whole seasons, as the levels increases the game hardness increases. The Doctor need to take the vaccine to the virus infected patients, and cure them. The doctor avatar need to give them the vaccine to that patient, so by delivering the vaccine, the patient he will be cured.

How The Game Works?

Seasons

There are two main seasons in the app one is Morning and the other is Locked. It will be unlocked one the player completes all the levels in the morning season. In the morning season, the doctor avatar need to deliver the vaccine to the virus infected patients and cure them.

Game

In the game, the doctor avatar need to deliver the vaccine to the virus infected patients and cure them. The player has two ways to control the doctor avatar, one is by using the joystick and the other is by using the touch screen. If all the patients are cured, you will win the game and move to the next level. If the player failed to cure the patiends in the given time, the game will be over,

levels

There are 5 levels in each season. In each level, the doctor avatar need to deliver the vaccine to the virus infected patients. As the player goes to higher levels, the game hardness increases. There will be timer in each level, if the player fails to cure the patients in the given time, the game will be over.

Timer

Based on the difficulty level, the timer will be set. Failing to cure the patients in the given time, the game will be over. At any time, the player can restart the game.
